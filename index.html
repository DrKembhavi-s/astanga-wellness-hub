About Astanga Wellness Hub

Astanga Wellness Hub is a visionary initiative under Astanga Wellness Private Limited, dedicated to elevating Ayurvedic education through structured training, mentorship, and digital innovation. Rooted in classical wisdom and branching into modern healthcare, our platform supports learners from foundational levels to clinical practice.

Our Programs

Courses

Structured modules for students, interns, and practitioners across all levels.

Assessments

MCQs, case-based evaluations, and OSCE-style tools to track progress.

Internship

Clinical exposure and guided practice with senior mentors.

For Practitioners

Skill upgrade modules, CME credits, and advanced diagnostics training.
