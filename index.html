About Us

Astanga Wellness Hub is a digital initiative to train and assess students, interns, and practicing doctors in Ayurveda. We offer structured courses, mentorship, and tools for professional growth.

Our Programs

Courses

Foundational to advanced modules designed for students, interns, and practitioners.

Assessments

Internship

Guided clinical exposure with mentorship from experienced Ayurvedic faculty.

Practitioner Modules

Skill upgrades, CME credits, and advanced training for practicing doctors.
